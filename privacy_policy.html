Privacy Policy — مُنادٍ (Munadi)

Last updated: March 2025

1. Information We Use

1.1 Location

We use your device’s location only to calculate prayer times for your area and to show Qibla direction. With your permission, the app gets your approximate coordinates (latitude and longitude). This is used locally on your device to compute prayer times. We do not send your location to our servers. You can revoke location permission at any time in device settings.

1.2 Local Storage

Your preferences (e.g. notification settings, language) and app progress are stored only on your device. This data is not sent to us or any third parties.

1.3 Notifications

The app shows local notifications for prayer times. These are managed by your device's operating system; no personal data is transmitted for this purpose.

2. Data Security

  • We do not collect or store personal data on our servers.
  • We do not sell or share your information with third parties.
  • No tracking or profiling is performed within the app.

3. Third-Party Services

If you use map features, data may be handled by Google according to Google’s Privacy Policy.
