# Privacy Policy — مُنادٍ (Munadi)

**Last updated:** March 2025

مُنادٍ ("Munadi") is a prayer times, Qibla direction, Quran, and Adhkar app. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app uses and how we handle it.

---

## 1. Information We Use

### 1.1 Location
- **Purpose:** To calculate accurate prayer times for your area and to show Qibla direction.
- **How it works:** With your permission, the app gets your device’s approximate location (latitude and longitude). This is used only on your device (or via your device’s system services) to compute prayer times and the direction to the Kaaba. You can deny location and use the app with limited features (e.g. manual city selection if we support it).
- **We do not** send your location to our own servers. Any use of system services (e.g. for reverse geocoding or maps) is subject to your device and OS provider’s policies (e.g. Google).

### 1.2 Data Stored on Your Device
- **Settings:** Your preferences (e.g. notification times, adhan sound, language) are stored locally on your device using the app’s local storage (e.g. SharedPreferences / Hive).
- **Progress:** Any progress for Adhkar or similar features is stored only on your device.
- This data is **not** sent to us or to third parties.

### 1.3 Notifications
- The app can show **local notifications** (e.g. for adhan and Adhkar reminders). These are scheduled and displayed by your device; we do not run our own notification server that receives personal data.

---

## 2. What We Do Not Do

- We do **not** collect or store your personal data on our servers.
- We do **not** sell or rent your data to third parties.
- We do **not** use your data for advertising or profiling.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

- **Google Maps:** If you use the map or map-related features, Google’s services may receive data (e.g. general location) according to [Google’s Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- **Device / OS:** Location and notifications rely on your device’s operating system (e.g. Android), which has its own privacy and permissions. We only use the minimum necessary for prayer times, Qibla, and reminders.

---

## 4. Your Choices

- **Location:** You can revoke location permission in your device settings. The app will then work with limited or manual location input if supported.
- **Notifications:** You can disable notifications in the app or in system settings.
- **Local data:** Uninstalling the app will remove the data stored by the app on your device.

---

## 5. Children

The app does not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided data through the app, please contact us and we will address it.

---

## 6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will be revised when we do. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
